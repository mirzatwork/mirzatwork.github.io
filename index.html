About Me

I'm a cybersecurity professional with 3+ years of hands-on SOC and security analyst experience, currently completing my M.S. in Cybersecurity at Pace University (Seidenberg School, GPA 3.8).

My work spans SOC operations, threat detection, malware analysis, network forensics, and ICS/SCADA security. I specialise in identifying attack chains that others miss — whether it's a Netcat null-byte probe, a poisoned ARP table, or a phishing document hiding shellcode inside a VBA macro.

I'm also the creator of @cyberguardianss — a cybersecurity education brand on Instagram dedicated to making complex security concepts accessible to the next generation of defenders.
